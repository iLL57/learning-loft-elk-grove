What We Are

A Gathering Place for Homeschool Families

The Learning Loft of Elk Grove is a part-time enrichment program designed specifically for homeschooling families. We gather twice a week to give children the hands-on projects, peer community, and creative exploration that make learning unforgettable.

We're not a school — we're a gathering place. A space where curious children roll up their sleeves, get their hands in the soil, make something beautiful, and discover what they're capable of alongside friends who truly get it.

What We Offer

The Learning Loft Experience

Five areas of enrichment woven together every session — academic foundations alongside creativity, nature, life skills, and community.

Academic Foundations

Small-group ELA, Social Studies, and SEL — taught with care and matched to each child's grade level.

Art & Creativity

Visual arts, maker projects, and creative expression every session — Tuesdays in the art studio, Thursdays in foreign language.

Gardening & Nature

Hands-on outdoor exploration, planting, and nature science — with the living world as our classroom.

Life Skills

Cooking, sewing, and real-world practical skills that build genuine confidence and everyday competence.

Community & Connection

Peer friendships, community lunch, and the sense of belonging that makes children skip through the door.
