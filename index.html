Our Story

Born from a Homeschool Family, Built for All Families

The Learning Loft of Elk Grove grew out of a simple desire: to give homeschooled children a place where they could learn alongside peers, grow in community, and experience the kind of rich, whole-child education that goes beyond textbooks.

We are parents, educators, and neighbors who believe that the best learning happens when children feel safe, seen, and inspired.
