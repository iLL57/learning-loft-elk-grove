Who We Are

A Place for Meaningful Learning and Lasting Connections

The Learning Loft exists to meet a growing need within the homeschool community by providing enriching educational experiences that complement the homeschool journey. More than an enrichment program, we are a community where children are inspired to explore, create, and grow through hands-on, project-based learning while building meaningful friendships and the confidence to embrace new challenges. We partner with families to cultivate curiosity, nurture confidence, and create a welcoming community where every child can thrive.

What We Offer

The Learning Loft Experience

Five thoughtfully designed areas of enrichment work together to cultivate curiosity, support whole-child development, and build a thriving community of learners.

Word & World

Strengthen literacy, communication, critical thinking, civic understanding, and social emotional skills through engaging, project-based learning experience.

Canvas & Curtain

Express creativity while reinforcing academic concepts through visual arts, theatre arts, musical expression, dance and movement, and hands-on artistic projects.

Roots & Research

Students engage in scientific inquiry through observation, experimentation, and outdoor exploration, including gardening and hands-on investigations.

Lead & Launch

Prepares students for success beyond the classroom by developing practical skills that promote independence, responsibility, leadership, financial literacy, communication, and problem-solving.

Speak & Story

Students are introduced to a new language through interactive activities, games, songs, storytelling, and cultural exploration.

  • Looking for a hands-on enrichment experience for children ages 5–12
  • Seeking peer community and friendship beyond the home
