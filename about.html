About The Learning Loft

A safe, welcoming space where children learn, build meaningful relationships, and thrive.

How We Began

Our Story

The Learning Loft was born from a deeply personal journey. When our family began homeschooling, we discovered both the freedom it offered and the challenges that came with it. We longed for a place where our children could build genuine friendships and learn alongside peers who truly got it.

"We weren't simply looking for a program. We were searching for a community."

We looked for programs that balanced real academics with creativity and hands-on learning, and found the options limited. So we built one, right here in Elk Grove. It's a place where kids roll up their sleeves, dig into projects, and build the kind of friendships that turn a homeschool day into something they look forward to.

Today, The Learning Loft is more than an enrichment program. We're a community where children are known and curiosity leads learning.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

These six values are not just words on a wall — they shape every rotation, every conversation, and every decision we make.

1

Community & Connection

We believe children and families thrive when they feel connected, supported, and seen. The Learning Loft was built to create meaningful relationships among homeschool families and provide a true sense of belonging within the community.

2

Whole-Child Learning

We value an approach to education that nurtures not only academics, but also creativity, confidence, emotional growth, social development, and real-world life skills.

3

Creativity & Curiosity

We believe children learn best when they are encouraged to explore, imagine, create, and ask questions. The Learning Loft fosters an environment where curiosity is celebrated and creativity is woven into everyday learning.

4

Real-Life Preparedness

Education should prepare children for life beyond the classroom. From social-emotional learning to entrepreneurship and financial literacy, along with hands-on experiences, we equip children with essential skills to grow into capable, confident, and compassionate individuals.

5

Safe & Nurturing Environment

Children flourish in spaces where they feel emotionally safe, encouraged, and valued. We are committed to creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where every child feels welcome and supported.

6

Purposeful Structure & Freedom

We believe children benefit from both structure and flexibility. The Learning Loft blends guided enrichment with creative exploration to support healthy growth, independence, and confidence.
